Cash Finance for Individual

Cash Finance for Individual

A financing product that allows individuals to fulfill their financial needs by financing a desired amount of cash through Islamic tawarruq while complying with the terms and conditions of the product.

* Conditions (For individuals):

  • Saudis:
    • Government sector employees.
    • Private sector employees.
  • Non-Saudis:
    • For those who work in government Sector or companies listed on the Saudi stock market only.
  • The financing amount ranges from 10,000 to 100,000 Saudi riyals.
  • The financing period ranges from 12 months to 60 months.
  • The minimum salary is 3,000 Saudi riyals.
  • Without a Guarantor.

Required Documents:

  • ID Number.
  • Salary Certificate.
  • Bank IBAN Proof or bank card estate the IBAN.
