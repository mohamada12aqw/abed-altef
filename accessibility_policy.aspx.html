Accessibility Policy



Accessibility Policy


This accessibility policy sets out information which details the availability of Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company’s website at the domain ALJFinance.com (the “Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website”) to users with different needs. This accessibility policy covers the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company’s aims and approach to accessibility, the core functions a user of the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website should be able to perform and what the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company is doing in practice to improve the accessibility of the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website.


Introduction


Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company recognises that many different forms of disability affect access to the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website. A proactive approach to improving the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website and the services offered via the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website has been adopted. Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company aims for the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website to meet the needs of disabled users and offer accessible information and services. Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company endeavours to move towards compliance with the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 1.0 AA standard. Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company seeks to provide a good experience for users of the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website with special needs.



Approach


Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company’s approach to accessibility is to attempt to make the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website and services easy to use and understand for all users, including by the customisation of computing devices. It is recognised that not just disabled people are affected by issues of accessibility. The Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website webpages intend to be clear with easy to follow content. Using the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website The Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website has been developed to be fully html 5 compliant with all titles, links, and other text content fully compliant. This allows all text readable by text readers and fonts adjustable by html 5 compliant browsers. The Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website is responsive to both form factor and font sizing changes assuming reasonable usage.
The Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website includes the following:

  • a site map.
  • contact details for Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company.
  • contact details to provide feedback regarding improving the accessibility of the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website.


Customer involvement


Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company welcomes the feedback of users of the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website with special needs. Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company will seek in the future to involve, as appropriate, those users in the development and testing of new services and content.



Alternative solutions


Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company may be able to offer an alternative format or solution to accessibility problems encountered by users of the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website. Please contact the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company using the contact details as set out below.



Standards compliance


Although the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website currently aspires to conform in all areas e.g., Worldwide Web Consortiums (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 1.0, Level AA, and ensures to maintain this standard once it is achieved. This accessibility standard is being achieved by ensuring the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website content is clearly presented and written in a way which the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company believes is easy to understand and follow.



Contacts


If you have any questions or feedback regarding the accessibility of the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website, or if you experience any difficulty using the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Website, please contact the Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company via aljufcrm@aljfs.com, giving details of your query.
