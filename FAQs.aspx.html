Frequently Asked Questions

We encourage you to review the frequently asked questions we received and our responses to them. If you have another question, please fill in the form here.

Leasing

What is leasing?
A contract under which the Company retains the leased asset (ownership of the car) until the end of the contract and the payment of all staggered lease payments. The Guest has the right to use the car in exchange for agreed monthly lease payments to the Company. The Guest is entitled to own the car after paying of all payments and all the terms of the contract which includes the Residual Value, or the Guest can return the car to the Company at the end of the lease term if he does not wish to own it.
Does the financial leasing contract comply with Shariah principles?
ALJ United Finance commenced Shariah compliant lease program since 1994. Our services and products are constantly scrutinized and vetted by Shariah Board. For more information, you may check the Shariah Board Approval of our Lease to own product using the following link Shariah Board.
What is the Residual Value or Final Payment?
The residual value or final payment is the amount for which you may purchase the vehicle at the end of the lease, if you decide so.
Is the financing percentage fixed?
Yes, it is fixed during the entire contract period.
Will you ask me for down payment in certain situations?
The monthly payment must meet the 30% of the guest salary. In case the monthly payment is more than 30% of the salary, we will calculate the difference as a down payment, as well as in some occasions ALJUF will request for a down payment when the terms are not complied with instead of rejecting the application.
Can I own the car before the end of contract? In addition, is there any amount that I should pay?
Yes, you can. However, you will have to pay the rest of the car value in addition to the three monthly payments to fund the cost of early closing of the contract after deduction of previous payments.
Can I also finance the residual value (last payment)?
Yes, you can refinance the last payment for 24 months, if the total original period of the contract and refinancing do not exceed 72 months in accordance with the terms and conditions.
What should I do when my leasing ends?
Once the current lease term ends and you have fulfilled your lease obligations, you have three options:
  1. Return your vehicle and enter into a new lease arrangement on a brand new one. This is our most popular option: It allows you to return your current vehicle, and if you qualify drive off with a new vehicle. For many customers, this option is one of the deciding factors for leasing - no worries with trade-in values or hassles with selling a used vehicle.
  2. Purchase the vehicle - Your lease agreement includes a lease-end Purchase Option Price. This allows you the flexibility to purchase the vehicle at an amount determined at the beginning of your lease. The company will collect any applicable fees and costs (registration, license, etc.) if you exercise this option.
  3. Return the vehicle - Another option is to return your vehicle and walk away.
What is the Residual Final Payment?
The residual final payment is the amount for which you can purchase the vehicle at the end of the lease, if you decide so.
What are the advantages of leasing?
  1. Lower Monthly Payments: Due to the mechanics of the lease to own arrangement which include a final payment, your monthly payments are 30%-60% lower than the installment module to rent a vehicle.
  2. Warranty Comfort: Most people like to lease for a term that coincides with the length of the manufacturer's warranty coverage so that if something goes wrong with the vehicle, the repairs are always covered.
  3. Lower Upfront Cash Outlay: The lease to own arrangement requires little or no down payment*, which makes getting a new car more affordable and frees up your cash. However, you can choose to make a down payment, or trade in your old vehicle, to lower your monthly payment amount.
  4. No Used-Car Hassles With leasing, the headaches of selling a used car are eliminated. When your lease ends, you simply turn it back to the company and walk away, unless you decide to buy it or trade it.

* Specific Conditions apply.

Can I buy my leased car before termination date?
Your financial lease agreement allows you to pre-pay and purchase your vehicle. If you wish to exercise this option, please contact the branch for additional assistance. This transaction needs to be facilitated through the branch. A voluntary early purchase option fee, together with title, and license registration fees shall apply.

Account Maintenance

How can I create or update my username and password online?
You can create or update your username and password through the site by following this link.
Can I pay via Credit cards?
Yes, you can pay by bank card or alternative payment methods such as direct deposit, bank transfer or payment service.
Can I specify the date for the monthly payments?
Yes. However, the new date should fall before due date.
Can I transfer my file from branch to branch or from city to city?
Yes. However, you would need to inform the branch.
Should I pay the monthly payment even though I do not use the vehicle?
Yes, the guest is obliged to pay the agreed payment on the dates specified on the contract, even though if he does not benefit from the leased vehicle for any reason.
What should I do when the contract ends?
  • Option 1: You have the right to purchase the car after fulfilling all the required payments and terms of a new purchase contract with the agreed residual value
  • Option 2: Return the car to the company, in which case the guest must return the car to the company intact, in working condition, with no defects and damages, except for which resulted into normal consumption and use
Are there any obligations to fulfill in the event of the expiration of the contract period and my desire to own the car or my desire to return the car before the end of the contract?
The Guest has the right to exercise withdrawal (cancellation of the contract). However, the following terms need to be considered:
  • The lease termination will be approved on the basis of the tenant's use of the leased vehicle up to a maximum of thirty thousand kilometers annually or 2500 thousand (five hundred kilometers) per month calculated from the meter reading at the conclusion of the contract. The meter reading is (50) fifty halal per additional kilometer
  • The price of lost equipment and accessories shall be calculated
  • Repair costs of any mechanical, electrical or structural damage are added
  • Any traffic or other fines are added to the calculation
In the case of any of breach on any of the terms and conditions mentioned in the contract, do I have to pay any obligations towards the company?
Yes, in some cases, you have to pay the value of the original cost (the cost of the term) in addition to three monthly payments following the month of cancellation. The value will be calculated based on the guest usage of the vehicle.
What is the mechanism of car retrieval in case of non-compliance with the required monthly payments?
The mechanism is conducted through specialized third party entities.
Is the company responsible for personal belongings when the vehicle is retrieved?
The company is not responsible for the loss of any personal belongings in the car in case the vehicle is retrieved.
Why is my file transferred to another branch?
Transfer of files may result to assignment to one of our collection centers in other locations. However, the guest could deal with any branch of the company, in any region in the Kingdom, or any branch is considered as representative of the company.

Other

When should I renew the driving delegation?
The delegation should be renewed every three months.
Can I take the vehicle outside the Kingdom during the lease term?
Yes for GCC and Egypt but excludes Qatar and Yemen.
What is the procedure to be performed when I cannot benefit from the vehicle?
The guest is obliged to notify the Company whenever a situation occurred that prevent the use of the vehicle completely or in part. You can contact the nearest branch or through the ALJUF CRM 8002442211
Can I take the vehicle outside the Kingdom during the lease term?
No. You must comply with specific terms & conditions that are set by the Company.
Where can I obtain information regarding any special incentives/finance plans?
Finance plans depend upon the season and differ with the make and model of the vehicle. You're welcome to see your nearest branch for specific details.
During the repair period, will the company provide me with a substitute vehicle?
No. Unless the workshop has exceeded the estimated time communicated to you to repair the vehicle or as a result of the manufacturer's warranty repair. Under these specific limited conditions the company reserves the right to monetarily compensate you.

Financing Tools

Will I be guaranteed on the amounts indicated on the Calculator?
The actual price and payment amount quoted on the Calculator is an approximation and is to be used as a financial guide only. The actual price and payment amount may vary due to many factors and does not include title and registration fees. Contact the branch for complete details.
