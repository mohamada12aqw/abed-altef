Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Co. (ALJUF), a closed joint-stock company under license by the Saudi Arabian Monetary Agency (SAMA). The company has a paid-up capital of 1000 million Saudi Riyals and provides vehicle financial leasing, consumer finance and capital equipment finance. We are pleased to serve you through our branches.

Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance is continually looking for ways to delight its guests and to be closer to them. In order to be accessible to our guests at all times ALJFinance.com was created to be available 24/7.
