E-services

1/26/2020

E-services

As part of its efforts to delight its guests through distinguished customer experience, Abdul Latif Jameel United Finance Company is pleased to announce the launch of E-services for all its guests through the company website.

E-services will allow the guest to do most of the after-sale procedures through the internet, with peace, comfort, anywhere and hassle-free without the need to visit the branch.

E-services requests will be processed through the guests account via our company website.

The new E-services that have been developed are as follows, and to find out how to use E-services, please click on the service that you need:

  • Istimara renewal. ( Click Here )
  • Transfer of vehicle ownership. ( Click Here )
  • Buying the vehicle. ( Click Here )
  • Additional driver - Add. ( Click Here )
  • Additional driver - Renewal. ( Click Here )
  • Additional driver - Remove. ( Click Here )
  • Printing authorization form. ( Click Here )
  • Reclaim Repossessed Vehicle. ( Click Here )
